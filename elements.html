How to Get Mobile Services

Step 1: Identify Your Needs

Determine what type of service you need:

  • New Phone Purchase: Visit retail stores or carrier locations
  • Phone Repair: Find authorized repair centers or independent shops
  • Plan Changes: Contact your service provider or visit their office
  • Accessories: Check retailers and carrier stores

Step 2: Search Your Location

Use the form above to find services near your address. Filter by type and distance to see options in your area.

Step 3: Review Options

Check the service details including:

  • Hours of operation
  • Contact phone number
  • Specific services offered
  • Customer reviews and ratings
  • Distance from your location

Step 4: Get Directions & Contact

Once you find the right service, you can:

  • Get turn-by-turn directions
  • Call ahead to confirm hours and availability
  • Ask about wait times and appointments
  • Check if they have what you need in stock

Where to Get Mobile Services

Retail Stores & Carriers

Location: Major shopping areas, malls, and carrier-owned locations
What They Offer: New phones, accessories, plan information, and upgrades
How to Contact: Phone or visit in person during business hours

Repair Services

Location: Local repair shops, mall kiosks, and certified service centers
What They Offer: Screen replacement, battery service, water damage repair, and diagnostics
How to Contact: Call for estimates or drop in for quick fixes

Service Provider Offices

Location: Carrier retail locations and service centers
What They Offer: Account management, bill payments, plan changes, and technical support
How to Contact: Visit during business hours or call customer service

Premium Service Centers

Location: Apple Stores, Samsung Service Centers, and authorized dealers
What They Offer: Manufacturer-authorized repairs, warranty service, and replacements
How to Contact: Call to schedule an appointment

Tips for Getting the Best Service

  • Call Ahead: Check hours and availability before visiting
  • Bring Documentation: Have your phone and purchase receipt ready
  • Ask About Warranties: Understand coverage on repairs and new phones
  • Check Reviews: Read customer feedback about service quality
  • Compare Prices: Get quotes from multiple repair shops if needed
  • Ask About Turnaround: Know how long repairs will take
  • Look for Certified Technicians: Ensure repairs are done by trained professionals
